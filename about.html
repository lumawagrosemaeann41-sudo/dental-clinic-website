About Us

Dolorfino-Cenal Dental Care is a trusted dental care provider established in 2025, in Barotac Nuevo, Iloilo City, Philippines. Founded by Dr. James Carr Cenal a experienced dental professional committed to promoting oral health, the clinic offers comprehensive dental services for patients of all agrs. With a focus on patient-centered care, the clinic combines modern dental technology with personalized treatment plans to ensure comfort, safety, and excellent outcomes. Since its inception, Dolorfino-Cenal Dental Care has grown to become a respected practice known for its friendly team, development, and adherence to the highest standards of hygiene and clinical excellence. Services include preventive dentistry, restorative treatments, orthodontics, and emergency dental care. Guided by the mission of improving smiles and enhancing quality of life, Dolorfino-Cenal Dental Care continues to build lasting patient relationships through integrity, innovation, and compasstionate service.
