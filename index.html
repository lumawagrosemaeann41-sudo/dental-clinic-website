About Us - Dental Clinic

About Us

Dolorfino-Cenal Dental Care is a trusted dental care provider established in 2025, in Barotac Nuevo, Iloilo City, Philippines. Founded by Dr. James Carr Cenal a experienced dental professional committed to promoting oral health, the clinic offers comprehensive dental services for patients of all agrs. With a focus on patient-centered care, the clinic combines modern dental technology with personalized treatment plans to ensure comfort, safety, and excellent outcomes. Since its inception, Dolorfino-Cenal Dental Care has grown to become a respected practice known for its friendly team, development, and adherence to the highest standards of hygiene and clinical excellence. Services include preventive dentistry, restorative treatments, orthodontics, and emergency dental care. Guided by the mission of improving smiles and enhancing quality of life, Dolorfino-Cenal Dental Care continues to build lasting patient relationships through integrity, innovation, and compasstionate service. We combine modern dental technology with personalized care to ensure comfort, safety, and excellent results.

Vision

To be trusted and leading dental clinic that provides high-quality, affordable, and compassionate oral healthcare, creating healthier and more confident smiles in the community.

Mission

To deliver safe, effective, patient-centered dental services and promote oral health awareness and preventive Care, to use modern dental technology and evidence-based practices, and to provide a comfortable and welcoming environment for all patient.

Goal

To improve the overall health and well-being of patients by providing comprehensive, accessible, and excellent dental care services.

Objectives

  • To provide preventive, restorative dental treatments with high standards of quality and safety
  • To educate patients on proper oral hygine and dental care practices
  • To maintain strict infection control and sanitation protocols
  • To increase patient satisfaction and build long-term relationships within the community
